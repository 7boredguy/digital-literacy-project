# Stay Safe Online — Safety Tips for College Students:

## General Online Safety:

1. **Never share your OTP with anyone** — Banks or any official agency will not ask for OTP on call or message.

2. **Use strong and different passwords** for each account and try to enable two-factor authentication.

3. **Avoid clicking unknown links** from WhatsApp, SMS or email — even if it looks like from someone you know.

4. **Check before trusting** — Always confirm identity of people offering jobs, rewards or refunds online.

5. **Keep your phone and apps updated** — Updates help in fixing security issues.

6. **Avoid using public WiFi for payments** — It is not safe for banking or money transactions.

## UPI and Financial Safety:

7. **UPI PIN means money goes out of your account** — You don’t need PIN to receive money. If someone asks for it, it is a scam.

8. **Don’t accept payment requests from unknown people** — Always check properly before approving any request.

## If You Are a Victim:

- Report quickly on **National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in**
- Call **Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930** (24x7 available)
- Inform your bank immediately to stop transaction
- File complaint in nearest police station
